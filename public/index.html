Game Instructions

Controls: Use arrow keys or WASD to move the snake.

Eat Apples: Increase score and length.

Golden Apple: Gives 3 points and 3 length.

Reverse Power-Down: Reverses controls for 1.5 seconds.

Avoid Obstacles: Hitting walls or obstacles ends the game.

Score: Try to get the highest score!
